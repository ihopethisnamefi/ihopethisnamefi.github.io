Biography

Web Developer

"Most good programmers do programming not because they expect to get paid or get adulation by the public, but because it is fun to program." - Linus Torvalds

What once started out as a burden during my Electrical Engineering Senior Design project turned into a true passion of mine. I've been developing code for over 9 years now and really enjoy the feeling of creating something that people can use and improve their lives with.

I've been fortunate enough to learn and be mentored from some brilliant minds during my time at Hewlett Packard and Hewlett Packard Enterprise, and consider myself blessed to work on and have ownership over many applications on HP's Supply Chain side to help both business users and outside suppliers connect.

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

"The more I train the luckier I get." – Renzo Gracie

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is without a doubt the toughest thing I have ever undertaken in my life. It has taught me numerous things over the years both about myself and others, but the most important is that putting in hard work and dedication is the only way to truly excel at something.

You can almost always find me rolling or coaching over at Eastside Austin Elite and loving every second of it.
